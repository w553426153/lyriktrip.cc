Reservation and Payment
1. Expert Consultation: After you submit your inquiry, our travel consultant will provide you with dedicated, one-on-one assistance. We will work with you to polish your itinerary and offer expert advice until the plan meets your exact needs.
2. Deposit & Fees: To start the bespoke planning process, we charge a $99 USD service fee for customization. Once you are ready to book, a 10% deposit of the total tour cost is required to secure your reservation and confirm your acceptance of our Terms and Conditions.
3. Balance Payment: Full payment is required 30 days before your trip begins.

Cancellation and Refund
It would be disappointing if you had to cancel the tour booking, but we understand it is sometimes necessary. LyrikTrip has a favorable refund policy to minimize your losses.
1. Customization Service Fee ($99):
If you have paid the $99 service fee for itinerary customization, please note that once we have started working on your plan, only 50% of this fee is refundable should you decide not to proceed. This amount covers the labor and time invested by our consultants.
2. Tour Cancellation Fee:
The cancellation fee will be calculated as a percentage of the tour total price as follows:
-- For a Small Group Tour:
- 30 or more days before the start date: No cancellation fee (except for actual losses caused by air/hotel/cruise cancellation).
- 29 to 15 days before: 30% of the total price.
- 14 to 3 days before: 50% of the total price.
- Within 2 days of the start: 100% of the total price.
-- For a Private Tailor-made Tour:
- 15 or more days before the start date: No cancellation fee (except for actual losses caused by air/hotel/cruise cancellation).
- 14 to 3 days before: 20% of the total price.
- Within 2 days of the start: 50% of the total price.
- On the arrival day: 100% of the total price.
- Yangtze River Cruise Exception: If your tour involves a Yangtze River Cruise, the cancellation fee follows the cruise company's policy: 50% of the cruise fare if canceled 16-30 days prior, and 100% if canceled within 15 days.
3. Refund Process:
If you need to cancel, please inform us via email. We will calculate your refund based on the date (Beijing Time) we receive your written cancellation. All refunds will be processed within 20 days of receipt.
Alteration of the Itinerary
1. For any changes made prior to the beginning of the tour, there will be no charge for our services. However, if there are any costs due to the reservation changes, you will be responsible to pay for this part.
2. After the tour begins, any extra charges caused by clients' changes will be added to the total. Any unused portion is neither refundable nor exchangeable.

Some hotels can provide the air fan on request.

6. Since most places in Tibet Autonomous Region are higher than 3,000 meters (9,843 feet), Acute Mountain Sickness (AMS), also called Altitude Sickness is the biggest health risk to tourists in Tibet. Visitors having record of heart, lung, other organ problems or anemia should consult their doctor before making the decision to visit Tibet. For more information, please check https://www.travelchinaguide.com/essential/tibet/mountain-sickness.htm.
