## How Payment Works

We believe payment should feel clear, proportionate, and trustworthy.

For many travelers, the most sensitive question is not simply *how much does this cost?* It is *what exactly am I paying for, and what happens after I pay?*

That is why we aim to make each step of the payment process understandable before commitment.

### Before Payment

Before any payment is made, we aim to clarify:

- the scope of the service being provided
- what is included in the current service stage
- what may require separate confirmation or third-party purchase
- how support is expected to work during the relevant period
- any important conditions related to changes, scheduling, or fulfillment

Our goal is simple: travelers should be able to make decisions from a position of clarity, not uncertainty.

### During Confirmation

Where applicable, we confirm the relevant service arrangement before proceeding so that expectations are aligned on both sides.

This may include:
- service scope
- timing and delivery expectations
- booking coordination logic
- traveler requirements or constraints
- relevant next steps after confirmation

### After Payment

After payment, the traveler should know what happens next.

Depending on the service, this may include:
- itinerary refinement
- service coordination
- booking-related assistance
- trip preparation guidance
- access to in-trip support or service communication channels

We believe a paid service should create confidence immediately, not ambiguity.

## Service Assurance

Travel is dynamic. Even well-planned trips change.

That is why our service is designed not only around planning, but around continuity of support.

### What We Aim to Provide

Our service approach emphasizes:

- clear communication
- practical guidance
- timely coordination
- thoughtful alternatives when plans shift
- human support when a situation requires judgment

### Before the Trip

Before departure, we help travelers reduce uncertainty through planning support, practical guidance, and better preparation.

This may include:
- itinerary clarification
- timing optimization
- location and movement logic
- travel preparation support
- identifying foreseeable friction points in advance

### During the Trip

During the journey, we aim to provide structured support when travelers need help navigating logistics, local tools, changing conditions, or unexpected issues.

This may include:
- route and planning adjustments
- local coordination support
- communication assistance
- backup suggestions when plans change
- guidance during time-sensitive situations

### When Circumstances Change

If a delay, closure, weather issue, coordination problem, or other disruption affects the trip, we focus on practical next steps.

Our priority is not only to explain what happened, but to help the traveler move forward with greater clarity and less stress.

### A Note on Scope

We believe trust grows when support is both strong and clearly defined.

For that reason, the exact level of service may vary depending on the service package, travel stage, location, timing, and the nature of the issue involved. Where relevant, support scope should be clarified in advance so travelers understand what level of assistance they can expect.
