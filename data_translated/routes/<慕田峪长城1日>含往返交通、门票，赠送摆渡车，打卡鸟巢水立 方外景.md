Attraction Overview
Mutianyu Great Wall (admission included) — located within Huairou District, Beijing, 73 kilometers from the city center. With a long history and splendid culture, it enjoys the reputation of 'The Ten-Thousand-Li Great Wall, Mutianyu Stands Alone' both domestically and internationally. The scenic area features overlapping mountains and vegetation coverage exceeding 90%. The Great Wall stretches 5,400 meters and is one of Beijing's famous Sixteen Scenic Spots, a national AAAA-level tourist attraction.
1. Upon arrival at the scenic area, proceed directly to the nearby hiking entrance — safer, more convenient, and less tiring!
2. Follow the guide directly to collect tickets. No need to queue for ticket purchase.
3. Tour the Great Wall for sightseeing, freely choosing your ascent method: hiking, cable car, or gondola.
【Friendly Reminder】: The Mutianyu Great Wall round-trip cable car costs 140 yuan per person, voluntary and self-paid.
Mutianyu Great Wall Bird's Nest Water Cube
