📍 21:00-23:00 | Natural Three Bridges
Suggested Duration: About 2 hours
Attraction Overview
Filming location for Zhang Yimou's movie《Curse of the Golden Flower》, UNESCO World Natural Heritage site, World Natural Bridge Cluster, and the world's second-largest tiankeng cluster — 【Natural Three Bridges Scenic Area】 (included: scenic area shuttle transfer fee of 40 RMB/person). Transfer costs between attractions are included (distances approx. 5 km between points). You may use scenic area vehicles or your own tour group vehicle. Fees must be incurred and paid directly to the scenic area — no discounts or refunds.
Natural Three Bridges formed millions of years ago, but only became widely known after serving as the filming location for Zhang Yimou's movie《Curse of the Golden Flower》. Three natural bridges formed by underground cave collapses, combined with two tiankengs, create the 'Three Bridges Flanking Two Pits' karst landscape. The scale of the tiankengs and bridges is exceptionally rare worldwide, delivering a powerful visual impact that leaves visitors in awe. Take the sightseeing elevator and descend into a valley over 100 meters deep in just seconds.

📍 07:00-18:00 | Longshui Gorge Fissure
Suggested Duration: 3 hours
Attraction Overview
Take the sightseeing elevator down to mid-mountain, then walk gradually along the stone steps on the cliffside to the fissure valley floor. Proceed along the rushing underground dark river, where waterfalls occasionally spray down and merge into the river beneath your feet. The canyon walls are lined with rugged rocks; looking up, you see only a thin line of sky. Continue slowly along the cliffside walkway, passing through the tunnel area. Inside, stalactites are densely distributed in varied forms — exquisite and magnificent. Press on, and after winding turns, you reach the canyon exit.

📍 07:00-07:40 | Zazidong
Suggested Duration: About 40 minutes
Attraction Overview
Zazidong (visit time approx. 30 minutes, excluding internal guided tours) was originally a small coal mine in Chongqing's suburbs, named for its abundance of slag and scarcity of coal. Surrounded by mountains on three sides and a ravine on one side, its location is relatively secluded. In 1939, Kuomintang Military Intelligence Bureau agents forced the mine owner to death, seized the coal mine, and established a prison here. Divided into inner and outer courtyards: the outer courtyard housed secret service offices and interrogation rooms; the inner courtyard's ground and basement levels contained 16 rooms for male prisoners, plus two flat-roofed buildings for female prisoners.

📍 12:00-18:00 | Yangtze River Cableway
Suggested Duration: 30 minutes
Attraction Overview
Taking the Yangtze River Cableway: The Yangtze River Cableway is Chongqing's second cross-river cableway, completed and put into operation in October 1987. It starts from Chang'an Temple (Xinhua Road) in Yuzhong District, crosses the Yangtze River to Shangxin Street (Longmenhao) in Nan'an District. As part of Chongqing residents' daily life, it has witnessed the city's transformation and is an indelible memory for Chongqing citizens. It is a unique landscape culture of Chongqing—a cableway spanning the Yangtze River—and has become one of the must-visit attractions for out-of-town tourists visiting Chongqing.
