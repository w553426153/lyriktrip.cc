Attraction Overview
The Wulingyuan Scenic Area is located in the northwest of Hunan Province and is a nationally renowned tourist destination, comprising Zhangjiajie, Suoxiyu, Tianzi Mountain, and Yangjiajie as its four major parts. Wulingyuan belongs to the rare sandstone peak forest landforms in the world. The scenery here has undergone no artificial carving; everywhere are stone pillars and peaks, sheer cliffs, ancient trees, mist and clouds, flowing springs and waterfalls, rare birds and beasts. Being here feels like entering a miraculous world and a naturally formed artistic landscape gallery. It is praised as the 'Natural Labyrinth'.

📍 13:00-13:20 | Bailong Elevator
Suggested Duration: About 20 minutes
Attraction Overview
The Bailong Elevator connects major scenic areas within Zhangjiajie National Forest Park—Golden Whip Stream, Yuanjiaje, Tianzi Mountain—into a single route. From near the Golden Whip Stream Grand Canyon to Yuanjiaje, hiking takes about 2 hours, while riding the elevator at 'Shuigua Simen' takes only 2 minutes to arrive.
The lower station of the Bailong Elevator is located near the 'Shuigua Simen' scenic spot in the Golden Whip Stream Grand Canyon (about 300 meters away), and the upper station is at the Yuanjiaje scenic area. The elevator has a vertical height difference of 335 meters, operating height of 326 meters, composed of deep wells inside the mountain and attached mountain well frames, with three elevators running side by side, each made of fully transparent glass. When riding, visitors can enjoy one of Zhangjiajie’s great views through the glass: the Divine Soldiers’ Assembly. The Divine Soldiers’ Assembly is located directly opposite the Bailong Elevator, composed of dozens of relatively independent stone peaks.

📍 15:00-17:00 | Yuanjiaje
Suggested Duration: About 2 hours
Attraction Overview
Yuanjiajie Scenic Area is a mountain plateau with lush trees, misty air, and overlapping peaks offering breathtaking views. The main attraction is the Qiankun Pillar, the filming location for the floating Hallelujah Mountains in the movie *Avatar*. Additionally, there are major sights such as the majestic Mihun Terrace. Mihun Terrace is one of the best viewing platforms in the area; standing on it, you are surrounded by hundreds of stone peaks rising dramatically, creating an awe-inspiring sight. The Tianxia Natural Bridge spans between two mountains, with a deck width of 2 meters, a span of 25 meters, and a vertical drop of 357 meters—making it the largest naturally formed stone slab bridge by vertical drop discovered so far in the world (visitors are not allowed to walk on it). There are two routes from the base of the mountain to Yuanjiajie. One is to reach the “Qianli Xianghui” junction in the middle section of the Golden Whip Stream Grand Canyon, then hike up the “Luan Cuan Po” trail to Yuanjiajie, which takes about two hours and means you’ll miss the scenery of the lower section of Golden Whip Stream. The other route is to exit the Golden Whip Stream Grand Canyon.
“Valley”, after passing through “Water Around the Four Gates”, arriving at the lower station of the “Hundred Dragon Sky Ladder”, taking the elevator up the mountain — the entire elevator ride takes only a few minutes. At the upper station of the “Hundred Dragon Sky Ladder”, there is a free eco-friendly electric shuttle bus provided by the scenic area to Yuanjiajie Parking Lot. It is recommended that general tourists choose this option, as it saves time and energy, and also allows viewing the latter section of the “Golden Whip Stream” scenery. After completing the tour of Yuanjiajie Scenic Area, visitors can take the scenic area’s free eco-friendly vehicle to the northern Tianzi Mountain Scenic Area for sightseeing. Alternatively, one may stay overnight in Yuanjiajie, as there are many farmhouses within the scenic area.

📍 11:00-15:00 | Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park
Suggested Duration: About 4 hours
Attraction Overview
Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park is named after the natural wonder ‘Tianmen Cave.’ When touring the scenic area, visitors must take a cable car approximately 7,500 meters long, offering panoramic views from the cabin of the winding ‘Road to Heaven’ clinging to cliffs and the mountain scenery below. The daring can also walk along the cliff-hugging, suspended glass walkway to experience the thrill of walking in mid-air.
Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park is relatively close to the urban area of Zhangjiajie. The Tianmen Cave in the mountain is a natural karst cave, and local people believe one can reach heaven from here. For ordinary tourists, Tianmen Cave is not open year-round; during winter each year, due to snow and icy roads, both Tianmen Cave and the Tongtian Avenue (a mountain road with 99 bends) leading to it are closed.
The lower station of the scenic area's main cable car is located in Guanliping, Yongding District, while the upper station is at the summit of Tianmen Mountain. In 2020, the scenic area opened the Express Cable Car, whose lower station is near the Tianmen Mountain gate and upper station near the Tianmen Cave parking lot.
Ancient trees tower throughout Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park, with vines entwining them. The scenic area is divided into several main zones: Tianmen Cave, Central Route, Eastern Route, and Western Route. Visitors can choose different routes and areas based on their preferences and physical condition.
The core attraction of the Central Route is Tianmen Mountain Temple. Near the temple is the 'Child-Seeking Cave'; folk legend says that Guanyin, the giver of children, manifested here, and sincere prayers can bring offspring. The highlight of the Western Route is a approximately 60-meter-long 'glass walkway' built on a cliff, with the entire path made of transparent glass. Walking on it, one sees the abyss thousands of feet below, feeling both terrified and exhilarated by the sensation of walking in midair. The Eastern Route focuses on primeval forest and now also features a 150-meter glass walkway.
Overnight accommodation is not permitted within the scenic area, so visitors must complete their visit within one day. Near Tianmen Mountain Temple is the 'Cherry Bay Restaurant,' which offers snacks and fast food.
Near the lower station of the Tianmen Mountain cable car, there are shuttle buses to the 'Grand Canyon Theater,' where visitors can watch the live-action performance and magical musical 'Tianmen Fox Immortal.' The story is adapted from the mythological tale 'Liu Hai Chopping Firewood,' recounting a deeply moving love story between a human and a fox spirit. On-site music, lighting, a giant moon, transforming fox spirits, falling snow, and aerial flying bridges create captivating scenes that vividly portray this legendary romance.

📍 13:00-14:00 | Tianmen Cave
Suggested Duration: About 1 hour
Attraction Overview
Tianmen Cave runs north-south, with a height of 131.5 meters, a width of 57 meters, and a depth of 60 meters. Rising from the earth and piercing the sky, it resembles a celestial gateway. This magnificent sight has attracted emperors, officials, recluses, eminent monks and Taoist priests, scholars, and poets throughout history to visit and admire. Over generations, this has accumulated into Tianmen Mountain’s unique and profound 'Heaven Culture.' The phenomena of 'Tianmen Exhaling Mist' and 'Tianmen Rosy Glow' are celebrated as rare and extraordinary natural wonders worldwide.
