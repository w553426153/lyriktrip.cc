Attraction Overview
Free activity in the scenic area for about 5 hours, fully enjoy the Great Wall scenery. Mutianyu Great Wall is located in Huairou District, Beijing, 73 kilometers from the city center, renowned domestically and internationally as 'The Unique Beauty of Mutianyu on the Ten-Thousand-Li Great Wall,' with a total length of 5,400 meters. Built in 1368 by Xu Da, a general under Zhu Yuanzhang, on the site of the Northern Qi Great Wall, it is the essence of the Ming Dynasty Great Wall, featuring Zhengguan Terrace, Jiajiao Tower, Yingfei Dao Yang, and other scenic spots. The wall remains well-preserved, excellently reflecting the ancient charm of the Great Wall. Former UK Prime Minister John Major, former US President Bill Clinton, and over 70 other foreign heads of state or dignitaries have visited Mutianyu Great Wall for sightseeing.
