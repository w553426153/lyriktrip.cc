📍 09:00-13:00 | Recommended attraction: Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Glass Bridge (Ticket not included)
Suggested Duration: About 4 hours
Attraction Overview
Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon is located in Zhangjiajie City, Hunan Province, integrating mountains, water, and caves into one, and is a museum of Zhangjiajie landforms. Here you can enjoy all the scenery of the northern temperate karst landform. Waterfalls and divine springs are everywhere in the canyon, and a tour along the way leaves visitors dazzled. The canyon is lush with vegetation, the air is fresh, cool and comfortable, and the stream is shrouded in a thin mist, as if entering a utopia, making worries vanish instantly.
The Zhangjiajie Glass Bridge is 430 meters long and 6 meters wide, with the bridge deck about 300 meters above the canyon floor, capable of accommodating 800 people. The entire bridge deck is paved with transparent glass, featuring a high bungee jumping platform at the center. The entire project has no steel reinforcement brackets and is a fully glass-structured bridge. After completion, it became the world’s first cable-stayed high-mountain canyon glass bridge.

📍 11:00-13:00 | Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park
Suggested Duration: About 2 hours
Attraction Overview
Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park is surrounded by sheer cliffs, majestic and magnificent. It preserves a complete original secondary forest, with rich plant resources, including the world-rare high-altitude Davidia involucrata community. The park has profound cultural heritage, with numerous poems praising Tianmen Mountain and many legendary tales, earning it the title 'Soul of Zhangjiajie.' It is famed for the 'Three Wonders': the Heaven-Reaching Cave, Heaven-Reaching Cableway, and Heaven-Reaching Road.

📍 13:00-13:30 | Tianmen Mountain Cableway
Suggested Duration: About 30 minutes
Attraction Overview
The Tianmen Mountain sightseeing cableway is 7,454 meters long with a vertical drop of 1,277 meters, consisting of 98 cabins, and a one-way journey takes about 30 minutes. The section between the lower and upper stations has a partial gradient as steep as 37 degrees—rare worldwide and the steepest in China. The cableway has three stations: lower, middle, and upper.

📍 15:00-15:30 | Glass Walkway
Suggested Duration: About 30 minutes
Attraction Overview
The Glass Walkway is a premium scenic route within Tianmen Mountain. The western section is 60 meters long and opened first; the eastern section is 150 meters long and opened later. The walkway is built on cliff edges, with the entire path made of transparent glass. Walking on it, visitors gaze down into the abyss below, feeling both thrilled and exhilarated by the sensation of walking in midair.

📍 17:00-17:30 | Tianmen Cave
Suggested Duration: About 30 minutes
Attraction Overview
Tianmen Cave is located within Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park and is one of the park’s most representative attractions. It runs north-south through the mountain at an elevation of 1,300 meters. The cave is
131.5 meters high, 57 meters wide, and 60 meters deep — rising abruptly from the earth like a celestial gateway. The phenomena of 'Tianmen Mist Exhalation' and 'Tianmen Rosy Light' are celebrated as rare and breathtaking natural wonders worldwide.

📍 11:00-14:00 | Fenghuang Ancient Town
Suggested Duration: About 3 hours
Attraction Overview
Fenghuang Ancient Town is located in the southwest of Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, Hunan Province. There is a mountain that resembles a phoenix spreading its wings in flight, after which the ancient town is named. Fenghuang gained worldwide fame due to Shen Congwen’s novel《Border Town》, attracting widespread attention. The ancient town’s blue stone slab streets, riverside wooden stilt houses, numerous ancient buildings, and rich Miao ethnic atmosphere together form Fenghuang’s unique charm. From《Border Town》to《Shen Congwen’s Autobiography》to《Xiangxing Sanji》, Shen Congwen created a literary cityscape with his plain yet flavorful words. Stilt houses, Cha Dong small alleys, and rope bridges with white pagodas have built the Fenghuang Ancient Town in the hearts of millions of readers — the Border Town in dreams.
