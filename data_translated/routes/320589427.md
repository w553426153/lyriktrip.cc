📍 12:00-17:30 | Badaling Great Wall
Suggested Duration: About 2 hours
Attraction Overview
【Badaling Great Wall】is a particularly exquisite section of the Great Wall, combining majestic grandeur with lush beauty, and ranks first among China's top ten scenic spots. In 1972, U.S. President Nixon remarked on the Wall: “Only a great nation could have built such a great wall.” In 2009, U.S. President Obama similarly said on the Wall: “Here, we must express profound respect for Chinese civilization.”
【Mutianyu Great Wall】is located in Huairou District, Beijing, 73 kilometers from the city center. It boasts a long history and rich culture, earning the reputation both domestically and internationally as “The Ten-Thousand-Li Great Wall, Mutianyu Stands Alone.” The scenic area features layered mountains and vegetation coverage exceeding 90%. The Great Wall stretches 5,400 meters and is one of Beijing’s famous sixteen scenic spots, designated as a national AAAAA-level tourist area.
1、 Since the Great Wall is far from the city (about 80 kilometers), early departure is required on the day of the visit—please prepare to wake up early.
2、 After ascending the Great Wall, visitors are free to explore independently; the guide will drop you off at the scenic area entrance, and no group commentary will be provided after ticket inspection.
3、 You may choose to hike the Great Wall or take the cable car/slideway; cable car fees are self-paid at 140 yuan per person.
4、 The default option is Badaling Great Wall; if you choose Mutianyu Great Wall, please make a note in advance.

【Qianmen Street】is located in the southern part of Beijing’s city center and is a famous commercial street. It hosts numerous time-honored restaurants and shops, along with regional snacks, making it ideal for shopping and dining. Additionally, the architecture along the street is ancient, complemented by archways, old signs, and red lanterns, evoking the authentic charm of old Beijing. The area also features many teahouses where traditional performances such as crosstalk and storytelling often take place—well worth a visit.
【Dashilan】Dashilan Commercial Street lies west of Qianmen Street and is a key component of the Qianmen business district. As a commercial street with nearly 600 years of history, Dashilan has long been a prosperous hub of old Beijing commerce.
【Fangzhuang】“Chinese-style Experience Street” appeals to young, trendy crowds while preserving traditional cultural vibes, completing urban renewal and upgrading to become a new cultural landmark in Beijing.
