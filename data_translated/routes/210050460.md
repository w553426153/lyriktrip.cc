World Natural Heritage Baofeng Lake, located in the core area of Zhangjiajie Wulingyuan Scenic Area, 1.5 km from Wulingyuan District Government. 📍 13:00-17:00 | Tianmen Mountain
Suggested Duration: About 4 hours
Attraction Overview
Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park is named after the natural wonder 'Tianmen Cave.' When touring the scenic area, you must take the cableway approximately 7500 meters long, where you can overlook the winding Tongtian Avenue on the cliffs and mountain scenery from the cable car. The brave can also walk on the cliff-hanging glass walkway to experience the thrill of walking in mid-air.
Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park is relatively close to Zhangjiajie urban area. The Tianmen Cave in the mountain is a natural karst tunnel, and locals believe one can reach heaven from here. For ordinary tourists, Tianmen Cave is not open year-round; in winter, due to snow and icy roads, Tianmen Cave and the Tongtian Avenue (99-bend mountain road) leading to it are closed.
The lower station of the scenic area's grand cableway is located in Guanliping, Yongding District, and the upper station is at the Tianmen Mountain summit. In 2020, the scenic area opened a express cableway, with its lower station near the Tianmen Mountain gate and upper station near the Tianmen Cave parking lot.
Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park is filled with towering ancient trees and intertwining vines. The scenic area is divided into several main zones: Tianmen Cave, Central Line, Eastern Line, and Western Line. Visitors can choose different routes and areas based on preference and physical condition.

📍 15:00-15:40 | Tianmen Mountain Grand Cableway
Suggested Duration: About 40 minutes
Attraction Overview
Tianmen Mountain Cableway is located within Tianmen Mountain National Forest Park, Zhangjiajie City. The cableway is 7454 meters long with a height difference of 1277 meters, and a one-way trip takes about 30 minutes.
The partial gradient between the middle and upper stations reaches as high as 37 degrees, rare worldwide. Tianmen Mountain Cableway has three stations: lower, middle, and upper. The lower station is in Guanliping, Yongding District, and the upper station is at the Tianmen Mountain summit. Riding the cableway allows visitors to overlook Tianmen Cave.

📍 17:00-17:20 | Tianmen Mountain Glass Walkway
Suggested Duration: About 20 minutes
Attraction Overview
The glass walkway is a premium specialty trail within Tianmen Mountain. Currently, three glass walkways are open: Eastern Line, Western Line, and Panlong Cliff. All are built on cliff edges, with transparent glass flooring. Walking on it, you see the abyss below, feeling both trepidation and the joy of walking in mid-air. In adverse weather, such as rain, the glass walkway will be closed for operation.

📍 19:00-20:00 | Tianmen Cave Day and Night Tour
Suggested Duration: About 1 hour
Attraction Overview
Tianmen Cave penetrates north and south, with a height of 131.5 meters, width of 57 meters, and depth of 60 meters, rising abruptly to the sky like a gateway to heaven. This magnificent sight has attracted emperors, officials, recluses, high monks, Taoist priests, scholars, and poets throughout history to visit and admire. Over generations, it has formed Tianmen Mountain's unique and profound 'Heaven Culture.' 'Tianmen Mist Spouting' and 'Tianmen Rosy Light' are hailed as rare global spectacles.

📍 11:00-13:00 | Fenghuang Ancient City
Suggested Duration: About 2 hours
Attraction Overview
Fenghuang Ancient City, located in the southwest of Fenghuang County, Xiangxi Tujia and Miao Autonomous Prefecture, Hunan Province, has a total land area of about 10 square kilometers. By the end of 2010, it had a population of about 50,000, composed of 28 ethnic groups including Miao, Han, and Tujia, making it a typical ethnic minority settlement. In 2001, it was awarded the title of National Historical and Cultural City, one of Hunan’s Top Ten Cultural Heritages. It was once praised by renowned New Zealand writer Louis Ai Liang as a beautiful small city in China, comparable to Lijiang Ancient City in Yunnan and Pingyao Ancient City in Shanxi, earning the reputation of “Northern Pingyao, Southern Fenghuang.” It is a National Historical and Cultural City.

📍 13:00-13:20 | Shen Congwen Former Residence
Suggested Duration: About 20 minutes
Attraction Overview
The Shen Congwen Former Residence in Fenghuang County, Hunan Province, embodies rich Xiangxi characteristics. It is a late Qing Dynasty building with over a hundred years of history, divided into front and rear sections with a small courtyard in between, flanked by ancient-style side rooms, giving a delicate and elegant impression. The flying eaves and sturdy gray walls are seamlessly integrated, appearing harmonious. The aged wooden walls and weathered, peeling doors and windows reveal the building’s antiquity and age.
