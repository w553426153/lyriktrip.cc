📍 09:00-10:30 | Du Fu Thatched Cottage Includes First Admission Ticket
Suggested Duration: About 1 hour 30 minutes
Attraction Overview
Du Fu Thatched Cottage is the former residence of the great Tang Dynasty poet Du Fu during his stay in Chengdu. At that time, to avoid the 'An-Shi Rebellion', Du Fu brought his family and wandered to Chengdu,
where, with the help of friends, he built a thatched cottage to live in, known as the 'Chengdu Thatched Cottage'. Du Fu resided here for nearly four years, creating many widely cherished poems such as 'Spring Night Joy' and 'Song of the Thatched Hut Torn by Autumn Winds'. The architecture here is simple and elegant, the gardens serene and beautiful, possessing both classical charm and a poetic atmosphere.
【Travel Tips (For Reference Only)】
1、Suggested Route: Main Gate → Grand Hall → Poetry History Hall → Chai Gate → Ministry of Works Temple → Shaoling Thatched Cottage Stele Pavilion → Thatched Cottage Former Residence → Tang Dynasty Relics → Thousand Poems Stele → Bonsai Garden → Flower Path → Shadow Wall → Daya Hall → South Gate
2、 Highlights of the Cottage:
√ Thatched Cottage Former Residence: Allows visitors to directly experience the living scene of Du Fu in those days.
√ Shaoling Thatched Cottage Stele Pavilion: 'Shaoling Thatched Cottage' is one of the signature landmarks.
√ Poetry History Hall: Displays Du Fu's poetic works and life experiences.
√ Ministry of Works Temple: Named after Du Fu's former position as an external official in the Ministry of Works, it holds commemorative significance.
√Tang Dynasty Ruins: You can experience some of the relics and scenery from the Tang Dynasty.
√Daya Hall: Features large colorful glazed murals and numerous statues of famous poets, very impressive.
√Flower Path: Red walls line the path, exuding a profound atmosphere, ideal for photo-taking and check-ins.
√Bonsai Garden: Bonsai art in various forms, showcasing the beauty of gardens.
√Screen Wall: Possesses unique cultural charm.

📍 13:00-14:30 | Wuhou Shrine Including First Admission Ticket
Suggested Duration: About 1 hour 30 minutes
Attraction Overview
Wuhou Shrine is not only an important cultural landmark in Chengdu, but also a key site for researching Three Kingdoms history and culture. It attracts numerous tourists and history and culture enthusiasts to visit, allowing people to feel that magnificent history and profound cultural heritage.
【Travel Tips (For Reference Only)】
一：Self-service Rental Guide Service: Rental fee is 30 yuan per time, requiring a deposit of 200 yuan per unit (visitors need to pay extra fees and handle procedures personally at the museum).
二：Cultural Relics Highlights
1. Tang Stele (Stele of the Temple of Marquis Wu of Shu): This is one of the treasures of Wuhou Shrine, full name 'Stele of the Temple of Marquis Wu of Shu in Shu'.
2. Shu Han Bronze Inscribed Crossbow Mechanism: The crossbow mechanism is 13.3 cm long, 6.7 cm wide, and 15.3 cm high, made of bronze casting.
3. Modern Chen Zhuang's 'Jin Guan City Outside Cypress Forest Dense' Horizontal Scroll: The painting is based on Du Fu's poem 'Shu Xiang' with the line 'Where to find the temple of the marquis? Outside Jin Guan City, cypress trees stand thick and dense'.

📍 15:00-16:30 | Jinli Ancient Street
Suggested Duration: About 1 hour 30 minutes
Attraction Overview
Jinli Ancient Street: Strolling through it, you can appreciate the ancient-style architecture and street atmosphere. Visiting various specialty shops along the street, such as traditional handicraft stores and snack shops, you can taste sugar-oil fruits, Three Cannons, and other delicacies. Walking to the middle section of the ancient street, there are some ancient theater buildings available for photo taking.
【Travel Tips (For Reference Only)】
The following are some food recommendations for Jinli Ancient Street:
1、 Three Cannons: Made from glutinous rice, after red sugar is steamed and melted, it is mixed with finely ground powder of fried yellow beans and sesame, resulting in a sweet and delicious taste.
2、 Beef Fragrant Crispy Cake: Made by wrapping beef filling in skin made from scalded flour with beef oil, then formed into a cake and pan-fried or deep-fried, resulting in a crispy, fragrant, and fresh skin.
3、 Spicy Cold Cake: Available everywhere in Jinli Ancient Street, with full color, aroma, and taste; although it looks very spicy, it is actually not too spicy.
