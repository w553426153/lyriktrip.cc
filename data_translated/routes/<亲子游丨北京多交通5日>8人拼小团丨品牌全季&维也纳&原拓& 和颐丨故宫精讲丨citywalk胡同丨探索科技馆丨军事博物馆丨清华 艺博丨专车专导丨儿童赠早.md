📍 14:30-17:30 | Forbidden City
Suggested Duration: About 3 hours
Attraction Overview
Explore one of the world’s largest ancient imperial palace complexes—the Forbidden City—includes admission ticket (3-hour in-depth tour), and experience the grandeur and luxury of imperial architecture.
The Forbidden City is located in the center of Beijing, formerly known as the Purple Forbidden City. Completed in 1420 during the Yongle reign of the Ming Dynasty, it served as the imperial palace for both Ming and Qing dynasties, an unparalleled masterpiece of ancient Chinese architecture, and the largest and most complete existing wooden-structure ancient building complex in the world. All buildings are divided into the 'Outer Court' and 'Inner Court,' surrounded by city walls. The moat, known as the River of Golden Water, encircles the city. Corner towers stand at each of the four corners. Each side has a gate; the main gate is the Meridian Gate (Wu Men) facing south.
